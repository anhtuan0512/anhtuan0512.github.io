    SAUSAGE PASTA SHELLS

    CREATED BY: ANHTU0512

    Serves Prep Time Cook Time
    3 8 MIN 28 MIN

    Ingredients

  • 1 package (16 ounces) SAUSAGE VGMEAT Brat Original (decased/crumbled)
  • 1 box (12.5 ounces) jumbo pasta shells
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 jars (24 ounces, each) marinara sauce
  • 2 eggs (vegan substitute)
  • 1 carton (15 ounces) vegan ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups shredded vegan mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 cup grated Vegan Parmesan cheese, divided
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 Cup Roughly Cut Spinach
  • 1 Cup Diced Onions
  • 2 Tbsp Fresh Basil (for topping)

    • Let's do it

  • In a skillet, cook and crumble Sausage VGMEAT over medium heat
  • In a pot, cook pasta shells according to package directions; drain and rinse with cold water.
  • Meanwhile, in a large bowl, combine eggs, ricotta cheese, 2-1/2 cups of mozzarella, 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, basil, onion, spinach, pepper and prepared sausage.
  • Prep your 3-quart baking dish with a coat of oil on the sides and bottom.
  • Pour half of the marinara sauce into the base of the baking dish.
  • Stuff the cooked shells with the combined mixture and arrange in baking dish.
  • Pour remaining marinara sauce over shells.
  • Cover and bake at 350F for 30 minutes.
  • Uncover; sprinkle with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Bake 5 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.
  • Sprinkle with parsley.
