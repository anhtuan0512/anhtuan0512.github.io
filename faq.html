    FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

    Our ingredients are simple and made from plants – without GMOs. VGMeat sources proteins, fats, minerals, flavors and colors, and carbohydrates from plant-based sources like peas, beans, potatoes and brown rice. More on VGMeat’s ingredients here.

    VGMeat is designed to have the same taste and texture as animal-based meat, so you can use it in your favorite dishes while feeling good about the choice you’re making for you and the planet. Learn more about all the VGMeat products here.

    You can find VGMeat products in over 112,000 grocery store meat cases, restaurants, and VGMEAT! Click here to see the latest Food Service partners or visit our locator here to find a store or restaurant near you.

    We use a simple process of heating, cooling, and pressure to create the fibrous texture of meat and layer in plant-based fats, binders, flavors, and colors. The result? Uncompromisingly delicious meat made directly from plants.

    Protein, fat, minerals, carbohydrates, and water are the five building blocks of meat. We source these building blocks directly from plants. Using heating, cooling, and pressure, we create the fibrous texture of meat from plant-based proteins. Then, we mix in fats, minerals, fruit and vegetable-based colors, natural flavors, and carbohydrates to replicate the appearance, juiciness, and flavor of meat. The result? Uncompromisingly delicious plant-based breakfast sausage, no sacrifice required.

    By using a variety of plant proteins including pea, rice mung bran and faba bean, our products deliver greater or equal levels of protein than their animal-based counterparts. For specific ingredients and nutrition information, please visit our product pages.

    Yes, the shipping box and insulation are curbside recyclable. Recycling guidelines do vary by location, so please check individual packaging and local guidelines for more information.

    We use dry ice which is contained inside a bag which is then placed inside its own designated box. Dry ice is a safe, common cooling material for shipping frozen items. When handling and disposing of dry ice, the outer packaging of the dry ice bag itself is safe to touch, but do not directly touch the dry ice inside the bag with bare hands. See the instructions printed on the bag or box for safe handling instructions.

    After removing the product from the shipping carton, place the box with the bag of dry ice back in the shipping carton and let it evaporate in the carton with the lid lightly closed. We recommend that you put it in an open area, ideally in an area outside and away from the reach of children and pets. Never touch the dry ice directly or put it in a sink, toilet, garbage, unventilated room, on top of tile or a laminate countertop, or in a glass or air-tight container.

    Please keep your product frozen until you’re ready to cook it. Thawing and cooking instructions for your products can be found on the product packaging. If you no longer have the packaging, please reach out to ethanandteam@vgmeat.com for instructions or you can find cooking instructions on VGmeat.com/products.
